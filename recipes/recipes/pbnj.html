Peanut Butter & Jam (or Jelly!) Sandwich

Description

It's pb&j, what else is there to say?

Ingredients

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • jam (or jelly!)

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on both slices.
  3. Spread jam (or jelly!) on top of the peanut butter on one slice.
  4. Put the two slices of bread together so that the filling is on the inside.
  5. Eat.

Jam vs Jelly - What's the Difference?

Jelly is made from fruit juice while jam is made from whole fruit. Also jam is great and jelly is terrible.
