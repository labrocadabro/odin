Bowl of Cereal

Description

It's cereal, what else is there to say?

Ingredients

  • bowl
  • cereal
  • milk

Instructions

  1. Put the cereal in the bowl.
  2. Add milk. Do not add milk before cereal. And for the love of God do not substitute with water, juice, soda or any other stupid idea you have. If you don't have milk, just eat it dry. Please.
  3. Eat the cereal quickly before it loses its crunch. Unless you like soggy cereal, you weirdo.
  4. Drink the leftover milk.
  5. Don't eat the bowl.
